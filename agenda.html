TAG 2018 Agenda

Planning for the capstone event in #PhilanthropyTech is heating up. As we speak, the Agenda Committee is reviewing the rich session proposals suggested by TAG members and partners.

Sessions will align with TAG’s customized learning tracks for seasoned IT executives. mid-career grants managers, business analysts, and more:

  • Management: Focused on leadership, technology management, change management, communications and collaboration
  • Technical: An in-depth look at tools and technologies including their functionality, selection, configuration, and implementation
  • Grants: Focused on grantmaking operations including tools, technologies, processes and management

Check back in mid-May for a finalized agenda.

  • Tuesday, November 7 — Opening Breakfast & Keynote plus Sessions, Networking Dinner & Exhibitor Hall
